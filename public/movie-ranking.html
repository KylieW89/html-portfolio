Ky's Series Ranking

Top Three Series of All Time

1. The Originals

Very dramatic series about the original vampires. Their story is unique and full of supernatural twists and turns.

2. From

People from all over the country have to adjust to life trapped in a small town with mysterious secrets. Follow the rules and you'll survive!

3. Stargate

An adventure across the universe, a team of specialists explore worlds and overcome many challenges, some known and some unknown.
